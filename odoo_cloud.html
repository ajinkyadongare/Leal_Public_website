OdOO/ERP Cloud

Independent ERP Business Change Advisory

Odoo ERP is a comprehensive open-source enterprise resource planning software that integrates various business applications into a unified platform. It offers modules for sales, inventory, accounting, manufacturing, human resources, and more, allowing businesses to streamline operations and improve efficiency. Odoo's modular design enables organizations to start with basic functionalities and scale up as needed.

OdooERP is our tailored ERP solution designed specifically for small and medium-sized manufacturing industries. It shines brightest in order-based manufacturing sectors, particularly in capital goods and processing industries. Additionally, it caters seamlessly to standard auto-components manufacturing units. OdooERP covers the entire spectrum of operations: from managing customer orders and internal production work orders to material requirement planning, procurement, quality control, and production processes. It extends to job-work outsourcing, final assembly, sales, and dispatch. Every one of these functionalities is both built-in and configurable to meet your unique business needs.

Additionally, Odoo's robust community support and regular updates ensure it remains a cutting-edge solution for managing and automating business processes.
